# Code of Conduct

TraceQuarry is a DFIR project built for careful, evidence-led collaboration. We
expect participants to communicate professionally, review ideas on their
technical merits, and make space for people with different backgrounds and
levels of experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Treat incident data, indicators, and research subjects responsibly.
- Distinguish verified evidence from hypotheses and attribution similarity.
- Accept correction gracefully and help others improve their contributions.
- Protect private, customer, and victim information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or sustained personal attacks.
- Publishing credentials, private evidence, personal data, or malware samples.
- Misrepresenting unverified claims as confirmed findings or attribution.
- Using project spaces to facilitate unauthorized access or harmful activity.

## Reporting And Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to
[contact@chillethicalpeople.com](mailto:contact@chillethicalpeople.com). Include
only the information needed to understand the issue; do not send sensitive
evidence by email. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict
participation, or take other proportionate action to protect the community.
