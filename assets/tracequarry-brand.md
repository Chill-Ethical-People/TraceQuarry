# TraceQuarry Identity

TraceQuarry is the product name for the local-first UAC forensic parser.

Tagline: Excavate the timeline. Preserve the proof.

Brand idea: A calm forensic workbench that excavates useful signal from layered Linux artifacts, then preserves that signal as defensible timeline evidence.

Logo concept: Layered quarry strata form a circular evidence field. The central twin-stem T represents timeline reconstruction, the lower circuit cuts represent parsed Linux artifacts, and the yuzu trace node marks analyst-confirmed signal leaving the evidence field.
